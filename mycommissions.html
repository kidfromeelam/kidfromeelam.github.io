COMMISSIONS

COMMISSIONS CURRENTLY CLOSED
Due to delivery and collection considerations, I only do commissions for those I know in real life, or people who can be given the painting through mutuals. Advance money of a third of the final amount is needed to cover canvas costs and ensure time and effort. The rest of the amount can be paid as I give you your commission

For Commission Requests, please email melaninpaintsbusiness@gmail.com with the following information:

Instructions

  • Desired theme of artwork - if to do with your culture, please send the colours/symbols/animals etc. assossiated with your culture for me to choose from. If you have a specific idea, then please specify
  • Desired photograph(s) and/or concept. Must be a clear photo/description to ensure accuracy of painting.
  • Canvas size
  • Time limit or completion date - I like to take about a month to produce a good quality painting, if there is a specfic deadline (eg. wedding/birthday gift etc.) then please let me know.
  • Below instructions are optional
  • Let me know of which artworks you liked of mine, for me to produce a similar art, if you need.
  • Let me know of any specific 'do's and don'ts' and we can discuss these

Please expect these painting commissions to be done in my painting style.
