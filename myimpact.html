Intro

All the *profits I make from my art go to charity. A cause really personal to me is the struggles my people have faced: the Eelam Tamil Nation, our Freedom Fighters and other communities from (ongoing) genocide and oppression. Visit my instagram to find out more about the issue. With my art, I wish to make money to help our people back home who are struggling - the unheard voices, struggling under the oppressive state and from the effects of the ongoing genocide.
*Profits are the money made from the artwork discluding the cost of the art supplies - I will only take into account the cost of the material painted on, eg. Canvases, and not the cost of the paints itself.

Donations to other charities committed to helping more causes can be viewed under "CHARITIES"

The Aramsei Trust Charity

The money we raise through my art will help our people back home in Tamil Eelam. This website will regularly update how much money is raised, and the information about how the money is used. Aramsei is an organisation that raises money to fund for children's education, help families live (who otherwise struggle due to the effects of oppression and what happened in the War against Tamils 1983-2009), by providing them with means to make their own money, such as providing them with farm animals, sewing machines, etc. as well as providing children who otherwise would struggle to go to school or people who struggle to transport, with bicycles etc. Money can also be used to fund projects that help our cause, as well as being used to buy food to distribute to people.
