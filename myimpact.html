Intro

All the *profits I make from my art go to charity. A cause really personal to me is the struggles my people have faced: the Eelam Tamil community, our Freedom Fighters and other minorities in the hands of the genocidal Sri Lankan state. So, with my art I want to make money to give back to the people who are still struggling - the unheard voices, struggling under the oppressive state and from the effects of the continuing genocide.
*Profits are the money made from the artwork discluding the cost of the art supplies - I will only take into account the cost of the material painted on, eg. Canvases, and not the cost of the paints itself.

Donations to other charities committed to helping more causes, that I've made with my art, can be viewed under "CHARITIES"

The Aramsei Trust Charity

Money goes to helping individuals/families affected by the Genocidal War/current oppression through eg. buying farm animals, funding for a child’s education, cycles for journeys to school, etc.
