ABOUT ME


Hi! My name is Nivega. I'm a Tamil self-taught artist based in London, England.
Nivega is inspired by nature, empowerment of BAME and oppressed communities and her roots as an Eelam Tamil from Eelam (search it up ! :)). Explore her artworks.
The profits from art sales will go to charities, where you can also view how much of an impact you've made through buying Nivega's art. We are striving towards creating art that is eco-friendly, as well.
