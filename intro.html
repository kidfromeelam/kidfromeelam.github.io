ABOUT ME


Hi! My name is Nivega. I'm a Tamil self-taught artist based in London, England.
Nivega finds calmness through painting, combining art and empowerment of BAME and oppressed communities. She follows dedication and passion from her roots as an Eelam Tamil from Eelam (who have a long history triumph and beauty, as well as ongoing oppression and genocide).
The profits from art sales will go to charities, where you can also view how much of an impact you've made through buying Nivega's art. We are striving towards creating art that is eco-friendly, as well.
