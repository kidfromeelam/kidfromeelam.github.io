ABOUT ME

Hi, I'm N. I'm sixteen, Tamil, and a self-taught artist based in England.
I've been drawing since the age of 1 (doodles), did Art GCSE and now continue to do art as a hobby and side hustle.

Baby me - yes, I picked up the kozhukattai (food) first

Wider Purpose

I make artwork to shine light on issues and empower communities, particularly the BAME and any oppressed community. I sell art to help my people, who are struggling due to the ongoing Tamil genocide back home, with the profits I make.
You can see the impact (and evidence of donations) we've made and how we can continue to help on "OUR IMPACT"

I hope my artwork is something you look forward to receiving, as well as knowing you're helping people.
