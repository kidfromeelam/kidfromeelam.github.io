ABOUT ME

Hi, I'm Nivz! I'm a sixteen years old, Tamil, self-taught artist based in London, England.

Baby me


I like to produce artwork that shines light on issues as well as art that empowers communities, particularly the BAME and any oppressed community.

All the *profits I make from my art go to charity. A cause really personal to me is the struggles my people have faced: the Eelam Tamil community, our Freedom Fighters and other minorities in the hands of the genocidal Sri Lankan state. So, with my art I want to make money to give back to the people who are still struggling - the unheard voices, struggling under the oppressive state and from the effects of the continuing genocide.

*Profits are the money made from the artwork discluding the cost of the art supplies - I will only take into account the cost of the material painted on, eg. Canvases, and not the cost of the paints itself.

You can see the impact (and evidence of donations) we've made and how we can continue to help on "OUR IMPACT"

I hope my artwork is something you look forward to recieving, as well as knowing you're helping people.

I have also made donations to other charities committed to helping more causes, which you can view under Charities but have made Aramsei - the charity I am working with - the charity that can easily be viewed on the website
